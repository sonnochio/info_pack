Understand Superpower Squad under 5 Minutes¶

What is SuperPower Squad?¶

SuperPower Squad is a Web3 fast-paced action shooter mobile game built on the BNB chain.

SPS Players will play heroes with unique abilities to fight in 6 different game modes with elements from Battle Royale, MOBA and RPG.

Secret Weapons 🔫¶

  • In-App Purchase & Contract Wallets

  • Customizable UGC Metacube Tools

  • Low Inflation & Burning System

  • 2 Years of Game Content, All Designed In-house

In-App Purchase & Contract Wallets 💳¶




Users can sign up wallet accounts with emails and purchase tokens directly with app stores

One email address can manage multiple devices (private keys)

Details can be found: https://docs.ssquad.games/

UGC Metacube Tools ✏️¶

User-Generated-Content (UGC): Users use Metacube and create NFT playmodes with SPS foundation to share or sell for profit.

Similar to custom games in DOTA, users can define rules, modify maps and assets.
